Target Audience

The target audience of the Infonado.org web site is described bellow:

  • Age: 11 to 30
  • Experience: Any educational levels.
  • Income: Any income.
  • Motivation: Wants to be in touch with many types of information useful for youth, but is not interest in reading a full article from a newspaper, and know useful day-to-day information as the weather.
  • How: It can be accessed from any desktop, laptop or mobile device with an internet connection.

Color Scheme

The colors chosen as primary reflect on the leaves from the icon, while the secondary ties in the idea of a calm environment (in contrast to the tornado pictured in our logo). The main idea is to make sure to anyone entering the web site that it's a good place with trustworthy information.
The accent 1 is a alarming color that will be used for alerts and things of sorts, as important announcements, while the accent 2 will break the calmary of the design sometimes to create interest.
